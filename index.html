Cauvery Hostel

Hostel Facilities

Single, double and triple seater rooms available. Each room has a study table and chairs. Spacious balcony provided. 24/7 security and Wi-Fi facility available. In-time for girls is 8:30 PM. Canteen and laundry facilities are available inside the hostel.

DO's

  • Mandatory attendance between 8:30 PM and 9:30 PM
  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas
  • Follow hostel timings strictly

DON'Ts

  • No loud music or noise after 9:30 PM
  • No outsiders allowed inside rooms
  • Cooking inside rooms is strictly prohibited
  • No damage to hostel property
